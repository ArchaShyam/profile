Job description

We are looking for a dedicated and enthusiastic Bakery Staff member to join our team! Your role will involve assisting in baking, decorating, packaging, and serving customers while maintaining a clean and organized workspace. No prior experience is required, but a passion for baking and great customer service is a plus! If you love working in a friendly environment and enjoy creating delicious treats, we'd love to have you on board. Apply now and be a part of our bakery family!

Responsibility

  • Assist in baking, decorating, and packaging baked goods
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the bakery area
  • Provide excellent customer service and handle transactions
  • Follow recipes and safety guidelines for food preparation
  • Monitor ingredient stock and report shortages

Qualifications

  • No prior experience required; training will be provided
  • Passion for baking and willingness to learn
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Strong attention to detail and cleanliness
  • Good communication and teamwork skills

Benefits

Competitive salary and flexible work hours. On-the-job training and skill development. Employee discounts on bakery products. Supportive and friendly work environment. Opportunities for growth within the company.
