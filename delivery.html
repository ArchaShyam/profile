Delivery

Thrissur, Kerala
Part-time

Job description

Join Paragon restaurant, as a Delivery Partner. We are looking for motivated individuals who are passionate about providing excellent service and ensuring timely deliveries. As a Delivery Partner, you will be the face of our restaurant, responsible for delivering foods to our customers with care and efficiency.

Responsibility

Responsibilities:

  • Interact with customers in a friendly and professional manner, addressing any questions or concerns they may have.
  • Ensure prompt and accurate delivery of food to customers within designated routes.
  • Maintain the delivery vehicle in good condition, ensuring cleanliness and reporting any mechanical issues.
  • Adhere to all traffic laws and safety regulations while driving and handling packages.
  • Work closely with warehouse staff and other delivery partners to ensure smooth operations.

Qualifications

  • No prior experience required.
  • Valid driving license with a clean driving record.
  • Basic problem-solving skills and the ability to handle unexpected situations.
  • Good communication skills.

Benefits

Competitive salary and flexible work hours. Employee discounts on foods purchased. Supportive and friendly work environment.
