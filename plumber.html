Plumber

Kottayam, Kerala
Part-time

Job description

Skyline is seeking a skilled and experienced Plumber to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing, repairing, and maintaining plumbing systems in residential and commercial properties. If you are a dedicated professional with a strong work ethic and a commitment to quality, we want to hear from you!

Responsibility

Responsibilities:

  • Install, repair, and maintain plumbing systems, including pipes, fixtures, and appliances in residential and commercial buildings.
  • Diagnose plumbing issues and provide effective solutions to ensure systems operate efficiently.
  • Perform routine maintenance on plumbing systems to prevent issues and ensure optimal performance.
  • Ensure all plumbing work complies with local building codes and regulations.
  • Communicate effectively with clients to understand their plumbing needs and provide exceptional service.

Qualifications

  • Working experience of atleast 1 year required.
  • Valid plumbing license needed.
  • Basic problem-solving skills and the ability to handle unexpected situations.
  • Good communication skills.
  • Ability to read blueprints and technical drawings.

Benefits

Flexible work hours. Supportive and friendly work environment.
