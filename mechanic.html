Mechanic

Kottayam, Kerala
Part-time

Job description

Aline motors is seeking a skilled and experienced Mechanic to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining a variety of vehicles and machinery. If you are a dedicated professional with a strong work ethic and a passion for automotive repair, we want to hear from you!

Responsibility

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct thorough inspections of vehicles to identify issues and determine necessary repairs.
  • Perform repairs on engines, transmissions, brakes, and other vehicle systems, ensuring high-quality workmanship.
  • Replace worn or damaged parts, such as belts, hoses, and filters, using appropriate tools and techniques.
  • Communicate effectively with customers to explain repairs and maintenance needs, providing exceptional service.
  • Adhere to safety protocols and regulations while working in the shop and handling tools and equipment.

Qualifications

  • Working experience of atleast 1 year required.
  • Valid license needed
  • Strong knowledge of automotive systems, tools, and repair techniques.
  • Ability to read and interpret technical manuals and schematics.

Benefits

Flexible work hours. Supportive and friendly work environment.
